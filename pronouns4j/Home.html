[HEADING=3]Placeholders:[/HEADING] Example format: He/Him/His/Himself
  • %pronouns4j_subjective% - Shows the "He" part
  • %pronouns4j_objective% - Shows the "Him" part
  • %pronouns4j_possessive% - Shows the "His" part
  • %pronouns4j_reflexive% - Shows the "Himself" part
  • %pronouns4j_pronouns% - Shows the basic "He/Him/His"
  • All placeholders can be manipulated with added "_upper" to make them capitalized or "_capital" to make the first letter of the pronoun a capital letter
[HEADING=3]Commands:[/HEADING]
  • /pronouns - The root help command
  • /pronouns set - Sets your pronouns
  • /pronouns view - View your pronouns
  • /pronouns clear - Clears your pronouns to the default (They/Them)
